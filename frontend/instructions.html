Story

The year is 2040. Technological development has radically changed the aviation industry, and air cargo transport has become an important part of the international economy.
You are a young and ambitious pilot who has founded your own transport company. Global companies and private customers trust you to transport urgent and valuable cargo, because your skills and efficiency are unmatched.
However, the world has changed. Climate conditions are more unpredictable, and airports compete with each other for favorable routes and airspace. Every journey is risky, and you are responsible for ensuring that important cargo shipments reach their destination safely and on time.
In the game, you are given different missions and cargo packages to transport. Every shipment can be critical: sometimes you're transporting a rare medicine, other times a hugely valuable piece of art or emergency aid packages to natural disaster areas.

Instructions

- In this game, you take on the role of a cargo plane pilot, responsible for managing the transportation of goods to various airports across the globe.

- At the start of the game, you will be presented with three different shipping contracts, each with unique requirements. You must carefully evaluate and select the contract that best suits your strategy.

- After choosing a contract, you can select and rent a cargo plane. Each plane has distinct characteristics, such as speed and fuel consumption.

- The primary goal is to successfully deliver packages while maintaining low carbon emissions and keeping time delays to a minimum.

- You lose the game if you cannot afford to rent a plane or refuel.

- You win when you achieve specific milestones, such as delivering a set number of packages, reaching a financial target, or completing an elite contract.
