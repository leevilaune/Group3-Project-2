Story

The year is 2040. Technological development has radically changed the aviation industry, and air cargo transport has become an important part of the international economy. You are a young and ambitious pilot who has founded your own transport company. Global companies and private customers trust you to transport urgent and valuable cargo, because your skills and efficiency are unmatched. However, the world has changed. Climate conditions are more unpredictable, and airports compete with each other for favorable routes and airspace. Every journey is risky, and you are responsible for ensuring that important cargo shipments reach their destination safely and on time. In the game, you are given different missions and cargo packages to transport. Every shipment can be critical: sometimes you're transporting a rare medicine, other times a hugely valuable piece of art or emergency aid packages to natural disaster areas.
